Avoided Emissions Dashboard

This project focuses on analyzing and visualizing avoided greenhouse gas emissions and related funding allocations. It provides interactive dashboards to explore and understand emission trends effectively.

Features

  • Interactive dashboards for emissions and funding data.
  • Visual breakdowns of sector-specific emissions reductions.
  • Comprehensive metrics for stakeholders' decision-making processes.
