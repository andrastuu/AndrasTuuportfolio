Profile

Data Scientist with expertise in machine learning, deep learning, and data analytics, specializing in Vision Transformers and CNNs for image classification. I develop high-accuracy models for galaxy classification using robust data cleaning and preprocessing. Skilled in social media sentiment analysis and data visualization, I use web scraping and Transformer models to create actionable dashboards in Power BI.

My experience includes work with Climate-KIC and Boston Consulting Group (BCG), along with research through my Master’s and Bachelor’s theses.

At Climate-KIC, I led data transformation initiatives, developed web-scraping pipelines, and optimized workflows to support climate-focused startups, enabling more efficient data-driven operations.

At BCG, I consulted on strategic analysis projects across healthcare, finance, and M&A. I optimized data platforms to support faster decision-making, enhanced accessibility, and led cost reduction and healthcare reform initiatives.

My Master’s thesis focused on applying Vision Transformers for galaxy morphological classification. By combining CNNs and advanced data preprocessing, I achieved significant classification accuracy improvements.

In my Bachelor’s thesis, I conducted a sentiment analysis of meme stocks, using NLP and Transformer models to examine the influence of social media sentiment on stock prices, providing insights into public sentiment’s impact on financial markets.
