About Me

Hi, I am a Data Scientist with a growing focus on automation and image processing. I firmly believe that data and AI technology have the potential to revolutionize the way we work by reducing the burden of manual, repetitive tasks, such as data entry, image classification, or sentiment analysis on large volumes of text. By leveraging these tools, we can free up time for more creative and strategic endeavors. I am a strong advocate for data-driven decision-making. At my previous employer, Climate-KIC, I developed and implemented efficient data management systems, enabling better information flow and more streamlined reporting through dashboards connected to these systems, and I contributed to improving forecasting tools and creating insightful dashboards using Python, SQL, Power BI, and Tableau.

My professional experiences have allowed me to gain hands-on expertise in automating processes, advising on innovative solutions, and applying financial tools like DCF pricing and PCA to solve real-world problems. For example, I consulted on an app for my home city, Budapest, as part of a project with the mayor’s office. Beyond my professional life, I am deeply enthusiastic about traveling, exploring new cultures, and embracing new experiences. I actively seek opportunities that challenge my comfort zone, as I believe discomfort fosters growth, learning, and a deeper understanding of the world.
